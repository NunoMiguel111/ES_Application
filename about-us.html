About Us

Welcome to our Drone Deployed Micro-Drifter project!

We are Team M, a group of 10 members from FEUP (Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto). Our goal is to develop a low-cost device capable of deriving on water to measure environmental parameters.
